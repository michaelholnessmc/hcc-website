🏠 Our Housing Communities

HCC currently manages three residential developments across Jamaica, each designed to meet the housing needs of different income groups. Click the links below to learn more about each community.

Birkshire Gardens

📍 Birkshire, St. Andrew, High Clere

One-bedroom & Two-bedroom Apartment Units

Birkshire Gardens is designed for low-income earners and young professionals looking for their first home. Located in St. Andrew, it offers modern apartments with easy access to major highways and the business district.

Min. Net Income$75,000 / month
Target GroupLow-income & Young Professionals
Age20–45 years
Unit Types1-bed & 2-bed

Features & Amenities

Modern kitchen fittings Built-in wardrobes Landscaped gardens On-site laundry Covered parking Highway access
Qualifying Requirements
  • Minimum net monthly income of $75,000
  • Age 20–45 years at application
  • Must not currently own property
  • Expenses + repayments ≤ 50% of net income
  • Proof of stable employment required

Bampton Villas

📍 Bampton, St. Catherine, High Clere

Two-bedroom & Three-bedroom Townhouse Units

Bampton Villas is a townhouse community in St. Catherine for middle-income earners and families. It features spacious open-plan layouts, private yard spaces, a community playground, and 24-hour security with a gated entrance.

Min. Net Income$95,000 / month
Target GroupMiddle-income & Families
Age20–45 years
Unit Types2-bed & 3-bed

Features & Amenities

Open-plan living & dining Private yard space Covered parking Community playground 24-hour security Gated entrance
Qualifying Requirements
  • Minimum net monthly income of $95,000
  • Age 20–45 years at application
  • Must not currently own property
  • Expenses + repayments ≤ 50% of net income
  • Valid government-issued ID required

Helmsley Court

📍 Helmsley, Kingston, High Clere

Two-bedroom & Three-bedroom Luxury Units

Helmsley Court is HCC's premium development in Kingston, designed for upper-middle income earners. It offers luxury finishes, backup water and generator access, an on-site management office with concierge, and is within walking distance of markets, transport, and healthcare.

Min. Net Income$125,000 / month
Target GroupUpper-middle Income Earners
Age20–45 years
Unit Types2-bed & 3-bed Luxury

Features & Amenities

Premium tiled floors & fixtures Backup water & generator On-site concierge Secure CCTV & intercom Near markets & healthcare
Qualifying Requirements
  • Minimum net monthly income of $125,000
  • Age 20–45 years at application
  • Must not currently own property
  • Expenses + repayments ≤ 50% of net income
  • Valid ID & proof of income required
